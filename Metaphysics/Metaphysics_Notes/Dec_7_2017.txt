1. Feser on god's intelligence
	a. Principle of causality
		1. every effect has a cause
		2. every actualization of a potential is actualized by something actual
		3. you can't give what you don't have
	b. Principle of proportionate causality
		1. effect in cause
			a. formally
			b. virtually
			c. eminently
2. Intelligence
	a. Understanding: capacity to grasp abstract concepts
	b. Judgement: combining concepts into complete thought
	c. Reasoning: inferring one thought from another
