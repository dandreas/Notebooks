=== Does the first cause have intelligence: ===
1. The first cause of intelligence must have intelligence.
	a. Any name that neccessarily implies limitations or potentials in its meanings must not be attributed to the first cause, and any meaning that does not imply these things must be attributed to it.
2. The first cause is the cause of intelligence
3. => the first cause must have intelligence
