2. 2 Desires:

		1. **Closeness**: sharing of deepest thoughts and desires
		2. **Union**: mutual sharing of deepest thoughts and desires
		3. **Full** **union**: mutually close, mutually present to eachother (physically, with attention)
	b. Good
		1. Has to be a desire for the other's true good.
